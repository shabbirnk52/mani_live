1. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide

If you contact us by email or through a website form, you may voluntarily provide:

  • Name

  • Email address

  • Phone number

  • Company information

  • Your message or inquiry

B. Automatically Collected Data

When browsing our website, non-personal technical data may be collected, such as:

  • IP address

  • Browser type

  • Device type

  • Pages visited

  • Time spent on site

  • General location (city/country)

  • Traffic analytics

This information helps us improve website performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

We may use collected information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries

  • Improve website quality and performance

  • Enhance security

  • Monitor analytics and usage trends

We do not sell or trade your personal information.

3. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to support basic website functionality and traffic analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

4. Sharing of Information

We may share information only with:

  • Service providers (hosting, analytics, security)

  • Legal/governmental authorities when required by law

We do not share data with external advertisers or marketing companies.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational security measures to protect your information. However, no website or data transmission is completely secure.

6. International Data Access

As a regional brand operating across Asia and Africa, visitors access our site from different countries.

By using the website, you acknowledge that your information may be processed in the United Arab Emirates.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your country, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data

  • Request correction or deletion

  • Limit or object to processing

  • Withdraw consent

To request information, email: customerservice@manifoods.com

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally.
Updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
