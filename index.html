Deliciously Healthy

Mani is a heritage brand with over 120 years of experience in nuts and dried fruits, built on a deep-rooted legacy of agriculture, food manufacturing, and innovation. Today, Mani is proudly part of AlHomaizi Food Industries, with exclusive rights across Asia and Africa, and manufactured in a state-of-the-art facility in Dubai, UAE.

From sourcing the finest ingredients from farms in the USA, Brazil, Spain, India, and Turkey to using advanced roasting (not frying) and laser-sorting technology, every Mani product is crafted to deliver quality, taste, and well-being in every bite.

We bring nature’s goodness to your hands through a colorful mix of roasted nuts, naturally dried fruits, and smart snack mixes, made for all moments of the day, from lunchboxes and gym bags to premium gifting and family gatherings.
